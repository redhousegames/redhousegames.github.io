Description

TiltShift is a logic-cube game that challenges your puzzle solving ability with a number of quirky blocks. Tron-inspired and bright with beautiful lighting, you will utilize a one-of- a-kind rotation mechanic to navigate the face of a three dimensional cube. Learn to see the 3D puzzle through multiple perspectives and think from steps ahead to raise your skills and solve seemingly impossible puzzles.

Features

  • A unique spatial manipulation mechanic to make arrow, pusher, toggle, and four-way blocks behave as tools rather than obstacles.
  • Beautiful lighting and concentration inducing music fill up a futuristic space.
  • Easy and intuitive puzzle navigation using swipes and taps.
  • Over 90 unique puzzles to solve.
